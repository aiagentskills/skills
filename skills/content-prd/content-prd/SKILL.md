# Content PRD

## Goal

Create a concise, structured content PRD that defines objectives, audience, messaging, deliverables, channels, and success metrics.

## When to use

- Write a content PRD for a campaign
- Define audience, messaging, and channels
- Outline deliverables for a content team

## Inputs to request

- Objective and success metrics
- Target audience and personas
- Key message and positioning
- Required deliverables and formats
- Distribution channels and cadence
- Timeline, approvals, dependencies
- Constraints (brand voice, legal, design system)

## Workflow

1. Restate goal, audience, and context.
2. Define scope and non-goals.
3. Outline messaging pillars and CTA.
4. List deliverables with owners and due dates.
5. Map distribution channels and schedule.
6. Capture approvals, dependencies, and risks.
7. Define success metrics and reporting plan.

## Output structure

- Summary
- Objectives and success metrics
- Audience and messaging
- Scope and deliverables
- Channels and distribution plan
- Timeline and approvals
- Risks and open questions
